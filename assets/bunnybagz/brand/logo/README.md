## Composition (verified 2026-05-05)
- Opaque pixels are dual-toned: black `B` outer + white bunny silhouette inside
- Eye dots are **transparent** (alpha=0), so backgrounds show through them
- Tight bounding box: (183, 189) → (715, 681), so ~22% padding around the mark
- Reads strongly on light backgrounds; the black outer ring vanishes on dark
  backgrounds, which is why the app ships a recolored `bb-mark-light.png`
  variant where every opaque pixel is forced to white
